Conclusion

Through these interviews I have learned a great lesson in having empathy for teachers and all they do in the classroom, especially when it comes to technology. Tehcnology is a definite educational asset that all kids should be exposed to and have the opportunity to learn about and utilize. However, trying to teach children using a format or resource that is unfamiliar can sometimes be frustrating for teachers. This is why the role of customer support at a company like Skill Struck is important. If teachers and students have the proper support, learning to use technology can be both stress-free and beneficial for everyone!

Crystal Colbert 2021
